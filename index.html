I'm Sofiia

Originally a humanities scholar,
turned into a techie by circumstances,
but always an artist at heart.

A little more about me

I graduated from an art lyceum in my hometown, but at the time, I didn't realize how much I would miss creativity in my life. Consequently, I took exams in humanities while simultaneously creating commissioned artwork for international clients. Later, when I moved to the Czech Republic, I embraced the idea of a new country meaning a new life, and decided to explore a technical field. This led me to study Multimedia and Virtual Reality Technology at CTU FEE. However, even there, I couldn't find my true calling.

It was during my second year at university that I realized how much I loved animating, drawing women, and cats. I decided that this passion should become my hobby, my work, and my life.
